History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley, aiming to discuss emerging technologies. Over the years, it has evolved into a premier global conference, expanding its focus to include artificial intelligence, blockchain, cybersecurity, and sustainable tech. What started with 200 attendees has now grown to over 10,000 participants annually, featuring keynote speeches, workshops, and networking events that shape the future of technology.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and drive positive change in the tech industry. We believe in bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world applications, empowering attendees to explore new ideas, build connections, and contribute to a more inclusive and sustainable technological landscape. Through diverse panels, hands-on demos, and thought-provoking discussions, we aim to equip the next generation of tech leaders with the tools and insights needed to tackle global challenges.

Notable Past Speakers

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, has spoken at TechCon multiple times, sharing insights on electric vehicles, space exploration, and sustainable energy. His contributions have revolutionized transportation and inspired advancements in renewable tech.

Satya Nadella

Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft, delivered a keynote on cloud computing and AI ethics in 2022. His leadership has driven Microsoft's growth in cloud services and AI, emphasizing responsible innovation in the tech industry.

Reshma Saujani

Reshma Saujani, founder of Girls Who Code, spoke about closing the gender gap in tech in 2023. Her work has empowered thousands of young women to pursue careers in STEM, promoting diversity and inclusion.
