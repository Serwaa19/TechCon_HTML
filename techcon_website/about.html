Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest tech conferences in the world, attracting thousands of attendees and renowned industry leaders.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon 2024 is to foster innovation, inspire creativity, and provide a platform for collaboration across the global technology community. We aim to empower professionals, students, and entrepreneurs to advance technology for the betterment of society.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the most influential figures in technology:

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Dr. Ada Lovelace, a pioneer in computing, spoke at TechCon 2018 about the future of AI and computational thinking. Her insights helped shape industry perspectives on machine learning.

Mr. Alan Turing

Mr. Alan Turing delivered a keynote in 2019 discussing cryptography and cybersecurity innovations. His contributions have influenced secure computing practices worldwide.

Ms. Grace Hopper

Ms. Grace Hopper spoke at TechCon 2020 about software development methodologies and the importance of mentorship in technology.
