History of TechCon

TechCon was founded in 2010 with a vision to bring together technology enthusiasts, innovators, and industry leaders from around the world. What started as a small gathering of 200 developers in San Francisco has evolved into one of the premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually.

Over the years, TechCon has expanded its reach, hosting events in major tech hubs including New York, London, Tokyo, and Berlin. Each year, we've pushed the boundaries of what's possible, featuring cutting-edge technologies, groundbreaking research, and inspiring talks that have shaped the tech industry.

In 2020, TechCon successfully transitioned to a hybrid format, combining in-person experiences with virtual accessibility, making our content available to a global audience. This innovation has allowed us to reach over 50,000 virtual attendees while maintaining the intimate, collaborative atmosphere that makes TechCon special.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, inspire creativity, and build connections that drive the future of technology. We believe that the best solutions emerge when diverse minds come together to share knowledge, challenge assumptions, and collaborate on solving the world's most pressing challenges.

Core Principles

  • Innovation First: We showcase the latest breakthroughs and emerging technologies that are reshaping industries.
  • Inclusive Community: We create an environment where everyone, regardless of background or experience level, can learn, grow, and contribute.
  • Knowledge Sharing: We believe in the power of open dialogue and the free exchange of ideas to accelerate progress.
  • Sustainable Future: We highlight technologies and practices that promote environmental responsibility and social good.
  • Global Impact: We connect local innovations with global challenges, fostering solutions that benefit communities worldwide.

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has been honored to host some of the most influential figures in technology. Here are just a few of the remarkable speakers who have graced our stages:

Dr. Sarah Chen

AI Researcher & Former Google DeepMind Scientist

Dr. Chen delivered a groundbreaking keynote at TechCon 2022 on "The Future of Ethical AI." Her research in machine learning ethics has influenced policy decisions worldwide. She is the author of "Responsible AI: A Framework for the Future" and has been recognized with the ACM Grace Hopper Award for her contributions to computer science.

Marcus Rodriguez

Founder of CloudScale Technologies

A cloud computing pioneer, Rodriguez spoke at TechCon 2021 about "Scaling Infrastructure for the Next Decade." His company, CloudScale Technologies, has revolutionized how enterprises manage distributed systems. With over 20 years in the industry, he's been instrumental in making cloud infrastructure accessible to startups and enterprises alike.

Priya Patel

Cybersecurity Expert & Author

Patel's TechCon 2023 presentation on "Next-Generation Cybersecurity" was a highlight of the conference. As the author of "Digital Defense Strategies" and a former security architect at major tech companies, she has helped organizations protect against evolving cyber threats. Her work has been featured in leading security publications and has shaped industry best practices.

James Kim

Blockchain Innovator

Kim presented at TechCon 2020 on "Blockchain Beyond Cryptocurrency," introducing his revolutionary SecureChain protocol. His work has enabled secure, transparent transactions across multiple industries, from supply chain management to digital identity verification. He's been named one of Forbes' "30 Under 30" in Technology.

Dr. Emily Watson

Quantum Computing Researcher at MIT

Dr. Watson's TechCon 2022 talk on "Quantum Computing: From Theory to Reality" captivated audiences. Her research at MIT has advanced quantum error correction and brought us closer to practical quantum computing applications. She holds multiple patents and has published over 50 papers in top-tier journals.

David Nguyen

Open-Source Advocate & Creator of DevTools

Nguyen's TechCon 2021 presentation showcased his DevTools framework, which has been adopted by millions of developers worldwide. A passionate advocate for open-source software, he has contributed to over 100 projects and believes in making powerful development tools accessible to everyone. His framework has been downloaded over 50 million times.
